About

I am originally from Meherpur, Bangladesh and currently working as a Research Assistant in the Active Robotic Sensing (ARoS) Laboratory under Prof. Edgar Lobaton. I earned my B.Sc. in Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology (RUET) in 2021, where my thesis focused on power electronics converter development. During that time, I designed and implemented multiconverters, multilevel and transformerless inverters, switching signal schemes, and interleaved boost converters and their control. I completed my M.S. in Electrical Engineering at North Carolina State University (NCSU) in 2024, specializing in power electronics converters, and I am now pursuing my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at NCSU (since 2022). My research interests span large language models, agenting AI, generative AI in cheminformatics, deep learning, and applied AI in robotics.
