About

I am originally from Meherpur, Bangladesh. I earned my B.Sc. in Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology (RUET) in 2021, where my thesis focused on power electronics converter development. During that time, I designed and implemented multiconverters, multilevel and transformerless inverters, switching signal schemes, and also worked on interleaved boost converters and their control. I completed my M.S. in Electrical Engineering at North Carolina State University (NCSU) in 2024, specializing in power electronics converters. Currently, I am pursuing my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at NCSU (since 2022). My research interests span large language models for knowledge hub development, generative AI in cheminformatics, deep learning, and applied AI in robotics.
